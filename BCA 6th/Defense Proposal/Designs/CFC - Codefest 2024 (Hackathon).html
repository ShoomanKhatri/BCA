CFC - Codefest 2024 (Hackathon)

Codefest 2024, organized by Code for Change, is the third edition of the biggest Hackathon in Nepal, which intends to build a technological solution for any of the selected 7 SDGs viz. SDG 3,4,5,9,11,13, and 16 , by the students pursuing bachelors in technology and the tech enthusiast students from +2 level in Nepal.

It is a dynamic event in which students who are highly interested in IT can learn new concepts, discover new ideas and garner knowledge in the domain of technology which will help them boost their interest and confidence in technology and help them shape their future in this exciting field.


The event will be conducted in 2 - phases:
1) Province Level: All the teams from a province will compete against each other and the qualifying teams from each province will be selected for the National Competition (Finale).
2) Finale: The winners from each province (total of 21) will compete against each other in the Grand Finale, which will take place in the capital city of Nepal, Kathmandu.

Prizes:
Winner of the Codefest 2024 will be rewarded with the prize equivalent to NPR 3 Lakhs 
1st runner-up will be rewarded with the prize equivalent to NPR 2 Lakhs 
2nd runner-up will be rewarded with the prize equivalent to NPR 1 Lakh
People's Choice award: 30k 

Along with various gadgets, services, and gift hampers.

* Each team can have minimum of 2 and maximum of 4 team members.
* Best practical ideas can also get chance of investment/incubation.
* Payment of the Registration charge (5000 NPR) will be communicated via phone or registered email.  

Event Timing:  Nov 17 - Nov 24 2024 [Provincial] and Dec 12,13,14 [Finale]
